auto_awesome The Soul Mechanic

"The soul is not a singular essence, but a currency of the beyond. To bind it is to command the very threads of existence." — Arch-Necrobinder Malakor

Souls are a unique resource for the Necrobinder class. Unlike Energy, Souls persist between turns but are cleared at the end of combat. Many cards either generate Souls (Harvest cards) or consume them for devastating effects (Blight cards). Managing your Soul count is vital for navigating Elite encounters and Boss battles.
