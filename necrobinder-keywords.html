Soul Weaving

Defeating enemies releases Soul Fragments. Soul Weaving allows the Necrobinder to stitch these fragments into their own deck during combat, temporarily enhancing cards with ghostly properties or creating ethereal copies of enemy abilities.

Harvest

Gain 1 Soul for every non-minion enemy defeated. Souls persist until the end of combat.

Stitch

Consume 3 Souls to add a 'Spectral Copy' of a card in your discard pile to your hand.

Spectral Dominion

A passive pool of Ecto. While above 50% Dominion, your Summons gain +2 Strength. Below 50%, your Block is increased by 25%.

Dominion Level 72%
opacity

Vitality Sacrifice

Ancient knowledge demands a heavy price. These keywords allow you to bypass Energy costs by paying in blood, or scaling power based on your missing health.

  • BLOODLORE Lose 3 HP. This turn, your next card costs 0 Energy.
  • SCARRED Gain 1 Strength for every 10 HP missing from your maximum.
  • EXSANGUINATE Deal damage equal to the total HP lost this combat.

Graveyard Interaction

Complexity: High
Exhume

Return a non-Ethereal card from your Discard pile to your hand.

Rummage

Discard 2 cards. Draw 1 card for every 'Spectral' card in your discard pile.

Burial

Select a card from your draw pile and place it directly into the Graveyard.
