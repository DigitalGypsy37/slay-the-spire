Reference Guide

Compendium of Symbols

The Spire is a place of arcane geometries and lethal signs. Decipher the iconography that dictates the flow of fate, from the blood of your health to the spark of your energy.

monitor_heart Vitality & Wealth

favorite

Health (HP)

Represents the player's life force. When it reaches zero, the run ends. Can be restored at Rest Sites or through certain relics.

toll

Gold

Currency obtained from combat and events. Spent at Merchants for cards, relics, and potion removal.

bolt

Energy

Refills each turn in combat. Used to play cards. Max energy can be increased through specific Boss Relics.

Extended Vitals

The combat interface displays current HP alongside active status effects. Icons like the Red Flag (Vulnerable) or Sword (Strength) indicate temporary modifiers that dictate damage calculations.

map Pathfinding Icons

question_mark

Unknown (?)

An encounter that could be an Event, a Combat, or a Treasure Room. High variance but potentially high reward.

swords

Combat

Standard encounter with Spire denizens. Rewards gold and a choice of cards.

storefront

Merchant

A safe haven where gold can be traded for power. Essential for refining a deck's archetype.

skull

Elite

Dangerous combat encounters that guarantee a Relic upon victory. High risk, essential reward.

fireplace

Rest Site

Allows the character to Rest (heal 30% HP) or Smith (upgrade a card). Other actions may be unlocked via relics.

featured_play_list

Treasure Room

A safe room containing a Chest with a Relic and often gold.

stairs

Next Act

The threshold of victory for the current floor. Leading to a boss or a higher elevation in the Spire.

skull Combat Status Effects

Weak

The target deals 25% less damage with Attacks.

Frail

The target gains 25% less Block from cards.

swords

Strength

Increases damage dealt by Attacks.

footprint

Dexterity

Increases Block gained from cards.

mask

Artifact

Negates the next de-buff applied.
