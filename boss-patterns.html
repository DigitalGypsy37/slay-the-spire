The Warden prioritizes defense in the early rounds. Utilize Artifact or Exhaust cards to handle the 'Dazed' clutter. When it enters Hyperbeam Charge, focus entirely on generating Block or use an Ethereal Potion. The stun window after Hyperbeam is your primary opening for high-cost attacks.
