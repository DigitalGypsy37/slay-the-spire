Greed vs. Safety

Elites provide Relics, the lifeblood of a winning run. However, the 'Path of Thorns' requires you to evaluate your current HP and damage threshold. Never enter an Elite node if you cannot survive a 'Turn 2' burst of 20+ damage.

payments

Resource Management

Gold is most potent at the merchant, yet its value increases as your deck matures. Save for 'Card Removal' to thin your strikes, and always keep a reserve of 150 gold for a 'Rare Relic' pivot in Act 2.

Boss Vanquishing

The Bronze Warden

Act 1 Guardian
precision_manufacturing

Known for its 'Thorns' and 'Stasis' mechanics. To defeat the Warden, one must pace their multi-hit attacks to avoid recoil damage.

Critical Strategy: Save a burst attack for the 'Summon' phase to clear the sentries before they apply 'Dazed' statuses to your draw pile.

Time-Eater Arkhos

Act 3 Ancient
hourglass_empty

Arkhos punishes 'Shiv' and 'Infinite' decks by forcibly ending your turn every 12 cards played.

Critical Strategy: Card counting is paramount. Aim to end your turn at card 11 to maximize your control over the 'Time Distortion' debuff.
