Vitals & Resources

The upper-left corner of the interface houses the most critical metrics for an adventurer. The Health Indicator (shown as 70/70) represents your physical integrity; depletion results in the termination of the current climb. Adjacent to health is the Gold Counter, tracking the mysterious currencies gathered from fallen foes and chests, essential for bartering with the Merchant.

Floor Navigation

The floor counter provides a numerical representation of your depth within the current Act. Each floor represents a node of conflict, rest, or mystery. Tracking this number is vital for predicting upcoming Elite encounters or the final Boss chamber of the stratum.

explore

Environmental Context

Beyond the floor count, iconographic markers indicate the current Act's theme. Whether traversing the ironclad halls of Exordium or the ethereal City, these visual anchors remind the traveler of the local fauna and hazards unique to that elevation of the Spire.

map

Cartography & Pathing

True mastery of the Spire lies in the art of Path Selection. The Map interface is a sacred scroll that unveils the branching destinies ahead. By interacting with the Map icon, players can analyze node types—ranging from standard combat and mystery events to treasure rooms and rest sites.

  • navigation
    Linear Advancement:

    Players may only move to connected nodes directly above their current position.

  • visibility
    Strategic Foresight:

    The map allows for zooming and scrolling, permitting long-term planning for late-Act synergies.
